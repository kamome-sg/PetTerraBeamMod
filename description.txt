This mod adds a Pet Terra Beam and a summoning item, and another recipe of Terra Blade!


Added recipes

Terra Blade, 10 Life Crystals, 3 Life Fruits -> Terra Blade(Summoning item)
Crafting station : Mythril Anvil or Orichalcum Anvil

Terra Blade(Summoning item) -> Terra Blade
Crafting station : Mythril Anvil or Orichalcum Anvil

999 Luminite Bars -> Terra Blade
Crafting station : Ancient Manipulator
